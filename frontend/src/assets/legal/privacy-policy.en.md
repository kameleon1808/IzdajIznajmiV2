# Privacy Policy

IzdajIznajmi processes your personal data in accordance with applicable data protection law, including the GDPR.

## What we collect

- Full name, email address, phone number
- Residential address (verified users only)
- Listing and transaction data
- Activity logs for security and fraud prevention

## How we use your data

- Providing platform services (search, rentals, messaging)
- Fraud prevention and account security
- Legal and regulatory compliance

## Data retention

| Data type | Retention |
|---|---|
| Chat attachments | 365 days |
| Audit logs | 730 days |
| Notifications | 90 days |

Account data is anonymised immediately upon account deletion.

## Your rights

- **Access** — request a copy of your personal data
- **Deletion** — delete your account in Settings → Account
- **Portability** — contact us to export your data
- **Objection** — contact us to restrict processing

## Privacy contact

For all questions about your data: **privacy@izdajiznajmi.com**
